Who Am I?

I am from Guangzhou, China and currently residing in the busy New York City.

Introduced to coding and cyber security since I was a junior in high school, I've developed an interest for web development and cyber security.

Not to seem like I'm only interested in coding, but I like to dabble in the arts as well. I've been quite the artist ever since I was young and I've definitely improved a lot when it comes to drawing. I've learned a lot about myself ever since I started drawing, such as, I really like simple designs and how anything is that more "simple" is so much easier to understand.
